TA (Teaching Assistant) Evaluation System (website based)

--- 29th PRP (Undergraduate Research Program) in SJTU

This website provides a platform for students in UM-SJTU Joint Institute to evaluate the work of teaching assistants in each course. Also it is served as a media to improve the working quality of teaching assistants in Joint Institute.

In this project, I'm mainly responsible for the front-end part. I design the UI (user interface) for this system which consists of three terminals: manager, teacher, and student. Also, I implement the interface with HTML, CSS and Bootstrap 3.0, and further implement the interaction events with pure JavaScript and jQuery.

You can find my work in ta.umji.sjtu.edu.cn/ta/evaluation (Corresponding IP: 202.120.46.151). This site is under testing, and you can visit it by revising the host file.

Evaluation Model for Eliminating Space Debris

--- work in 2016 Mathematical Contest in Modeling [Honorable Mention]

In this contest, my team figure out two methods to eliminate the space debris in the space: water jet model and net model. With these two basic physical models, we further develop an evaluation model based on AHP (Analytical Hierarchy Process) to evaluate those two methods. Finally, we implement our ideas with MATLAB to conduct model simulation.

I am served as the group leader of this project, and I am mainly responsible for the establishment and the implementation of the evaluation model. You can get our whole report here. Our group is awarded as Honorable Mention in this contest, and you can get our certificate here.




Polynomial Actuation

--- course project in VG100 (Introduction to Engineering)

In the winter of 2014, our team built the polynomial actuation as the project in course Vg100-Introduction to engineering. This device can be applied to the mould manufacturing to improve the efficiency and cost in the process of model manufacturing. The polynomial actuation consists of 7 stepping motors and a rubber surface, which can freely change the mould shape in an efficient and convenient way.

I am mainly responsible for the design of the physical structure. And you can find our engineering report here.
